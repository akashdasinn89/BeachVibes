PARA SAILING


Parasailing is a popular kiting activity among the adventure sports lovers in India. Parasailing isn’t just a water sport. The dictionary defines it as, soaring in the sky and free-floating while being harnessed to a parachute, which is towed by a motorboat, car or other fast-moving vehicles. With a medley of immeasurable adrenaline rush at the side of tuned seashore landscapes, is a frolicking sight to a lover of adventure and amusing.Parasailing is a popular aero sport among youngsters, adventure enthusiasts as well as office-goers. Parasailing or parascending is a recreational activity where a person glides through the air attached to a specially designed parachute, known as a parasail while being towed by a vehicle. The fun part is the truly magical aerial view and the momentary sense of flying up in the air. The flight distance can go up to 1 kilometre. Duration depends on weather conditions(wind speed and direction) and the body weight of the participant. The activity is suitable for beginners as well as experienced. Before the start of the activity, the experienced guides and instructors will introduce you to the safety measures and instructions about flying.Maximum weight restrictions apply for this activity; generally only customers that are within 100 kg may participate. It will be up to the Instructor to determine specific cut-offs on any given day, as these can vary according to winds. The total duration of the activity is Approx 10 minutes and is inclusive of briefing by instructors, strapping on and off of safety harness, and actual flying time. Actual flying distance(Ground Length) is 600m to 800m & duration will be between 1 -2 minutes depending on wind velocity and body weight. The duration of flight depends on the total runway length in wind direction. The actual flying time might be reduced, subject to the instructor's judgement, in case of bad weather or technical difficulties. No refund will be provided in such cases. In the event of any unforeseen weather circumstances/restrictions by Air traffic Control, the organiser reserves the right to cancel the activity. Upon landing, you will be met by an instructor to help you unstrap the parachute. The activity is not recommended for pregnant women, people with heart/ respiratory/ sinus problem/ claustrophobia/Back or Neck injuries or any other physical injury due to safety reasons. Please inform the crew/instructor if you have any medical problem.

HIGHLIGHTS

  1. Firstly you have to decide how many people are going to get on board: 1,2 or 3. Most importantly it is always more fun to go accompanied, or if you go with your family to do it with the children.


  2. The only thing that the person who carries out the activity has to contribute is the will to fly and not have vertigo or fear of heights.


  3. The passengers always wear life jackets and the harness is always equipped with a quick-release carabiners for an emergency releases; so that in the worst case scenario you can land as if it were a normal parachute.


  4. It can be practiced from 5 years of age, as long as the child weighs more than 25kg and is accompanied by an adult.


  5. It is not necessary to have previous experience, firstly the instructor will inform you of everything necessary to carry out the activity and enjoy it one hundred percent.


  6. The duration depends on the circumstances, it is agreed in advance with the instructor and it is usually about 10, 15 or 20 minutes per flight.


  7. Not recommended for pregnant women, people with heart ailments or serious medical conditions.
